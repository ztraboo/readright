Privacy Notice for ReadRight 

At ReadRight, we take your privacy seriously. This notice explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when using our app.

1. Information We Collect

User Information: Basic details such as your name, username, and email address to create and manage your account.

Student Progress Data: Reading progress, completed words, scores, and top struggled words to provide personalized learning experiences.

Audio Recordings: Audio clips recorded when practicing reading aloud to help track pronunciation and fluency. Audio clips are only stored if the “Enable Audio Retention” option is selected.

App Usage Data: Non-identifiable usage information to improve app performance and features.

2. How We Use Your Information

To track student progress and provide meaningful feedback.

To display learning materials and word lists tailored to your learning level.

To analyze audio recordings for reading assessment and improvement, if retained.

To improve the functionality and performance of ReadRight.

3. Audio Retention

Audio recordings are stored only if the “Enable Audio Retention” option is selected.

Audio clips are retained indefinitely unless asked to be removed.

Audio is used solely for assessing reading skills and providing feedback.

4. How We Protect Your Information

All personal, progress, and audio data is stored securely using Firestore, with access restricted to authorized users.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Sharing

Your data may be shared with educators or guardians to provide insights on student progress.

Aggregate and anonymized data may be used for research or app improvement purposes.

6. Your Choices

You can review, update, or delete your account and data by contacting us through the app.

You may enable or disable the Audio Retention option at any time.

By using ReadRight, you consent to the collection and use of your information, including retained audio recordings, as described in this notice.
